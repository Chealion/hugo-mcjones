### Dino Balidoy
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N1__9Xu5jBY

A modified version of the 4:6 method (brewed 92C and lower temp on last pour), and agitate at end.

1:14 ratio
20g coffee
280g water

Aim for 3 minute brew time.

1. Bloom with 60g, let sit for 30 seconds
2. Second pour of 50g
3. Third pour of 60g
4. Fourth pour of 50g
5. Fifth pour (80C) of 60g, agitate
