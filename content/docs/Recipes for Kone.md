# Recipes for Kone
## Go To
26g coffee
420g water

26g finer than paper filter  
Pour 100g water to soak grinds  
Allow to settle  
320g more water  
~2-3mL per second in centre

## Large
45g  
Pour 100mL to soak  
Allow to settle  
550 mL more water (~3 minutes to pour)  
Let drip for 1 minute
