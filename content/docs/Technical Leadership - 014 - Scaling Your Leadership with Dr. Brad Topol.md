# Technical Leadership - 014 - Scaling Your Leadership with Dr. Brad Topol

* 2:00 - the importance of always learning something new
* 19:08 - Constraint based leadership. Command and control when you know enough only works for so long. You will not be the smartest one in the room with time or just new/different technologies.
  * Give teams constraints - not a design. Ask/review, not direct. This allows things to scale.
  * When it gets stuck, then you can dig in.
  * Learn enough about the solution to be able to praise it up the org
  * Aka outcomes focused
